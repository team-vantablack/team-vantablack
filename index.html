WHAT IS F1 IN SCHOOLS?

  • STEM Challenge held worldwide in over 45 different countries
  • Includes Teams of 3-6 students between the age group 9-19
  • Students Have to design & manufacture miniature F1 cars using CAD/CAM tools
  • Car is then raced on a 20 meter track, powered by a CO2 canister
  • Teams also have to be ready with their team portfolios, and a trade display, which will also be judged
  • Teams are required to raise sponsorships in order to fund their project.
